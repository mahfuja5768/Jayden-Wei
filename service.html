With so many different types of debit cards available, it can be hard to choose the right one for you.

Standard Debit Cards

Standard debit cards are issued by a bank or financial institution, and are connected directly to a checking or savings account that you already have with them. The most common type of standard debit card is the Visa or Mastercard logo cards. With these types of debit cards, money is taken directly from your checking or savings account whenever you make a purchase using the card.

Prepaid Debit Cards

Prepaid debit cards are not connected to any existing bank accounts but instead are funded through deposits made by the user into the card’s own account. Prepaid debit cards often come with lower fees than standard debit cards, but they also offer fewer rewards and protections against fraud or theft than standard debit cards do.
